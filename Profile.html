Featured Farmers

Salman Johnson — Coming from St. Louis, Salmam Johnson's has been growing fruits and vegetables his whole life. He comes from a long line of farmers and is said to have soil flowing through his veins. Caring for and providing the best produce to his loyal customers, and he hopes to make you one of them.

Joe Dugon — Born and raised in Kansas, Joe Dugon is Salmans best friend. He has dedicated his life to raising and caring for livestock. With 30 years of knowledge under his belt, Dugon always carries the highest quality produce. He always claims that the secret to his success stems from the personal relationships he has with each of his animals, and lots of coffee.
